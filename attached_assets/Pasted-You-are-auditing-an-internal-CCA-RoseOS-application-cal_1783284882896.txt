You are auditing an internal CCA / RoseOS application called:

ExamsManagerOS

Your job is to perform a complete product, workflow, architecture, UI/UX, data, automation, and integration audit to determine whether ExamsManagerOS has everything it needs to function as a serious internal exam-management operating system for Contractor Compliance Authority.

Do not rebuild the app unless explicitly asked. First audit it. Identify what exists, what is missing, what is incomplete, what is fake/nonfunctional, what is risky, and what should be added before this can be considered ready for internal use.

This app should support CCA’s licensing and compliance operations where exams, qualifiers, study prep, exam eligibility, exam scheduling, passed exams, transcripts, reciprocity dependencies, and board/application readiness are critical blockers.

The audit must be detailed, honest, and practical.

---

## 1. Core Product Definition to Audit Against

ExamsManagerOS should be a RoseOS ecosystem app that manages exam-related workflows for contractor licensing and compliance matters.

It should help CCA track:

- Clients who require exams
- Qualifiers / individuals taking exams
- License applications waiting on exams
- Exam type required
- Jurisdiction/state
- Board or agency requirement
- Exam provider
- Eligibility requirements
- Study/prep status
- Exam registration status
- Scheduled exam date
- Exam result
- Transcript/certification status
- Reciprocity dependency
- Client communication
- Internal follow-up cadence
- Risk, blockers, and escalation items

The app should make it immediately clear:

- Who needs to take an exam
- What exam is required
- Why the exam matters
- Which application(s) are blocked by the exam
- Whether the person is eligible to schedule
- Whether study materials were sent
- Whether the exam is scheduled
- Whether the exam was passed
- Whether the transcript was ordered
- Whether the transcript was received
- Which states/applications can proceed after the exam result
- Who owns the next action
- What is overdue or at risk

## 2. RoseOS Ecosystem Placement

Audit whether the app clearly positions itself inside the RoseOS ecosystem.

Expected ecosystem relationship:

RoseOS
- Company-wide operating system
- Leadership visibility layer
- Department app hub
- Automation governance layer
- Source-of-truth coordination layer
- Internal workflow command center

ExamsManagerOS
- RoseOS specialized exam-management app
- Tracks exam requirements, scheduling, readiness, results, transcripts, and blocked applications
- Supports licensing, qualifier, and application-processing workflows
- Feeds risk, blockers, and next actions into RoseOS / Command Center visibility

Connected or future-connected systems:

Doc Collection
- Source for exam-related documents, IDs, authorization letters, board notices, score reports, transcripts, study receipts, and uploaded exam documentation

Application Processing / Licensing Workflow
- Source or destination for applications blocked by exam requirements

ComplianceConnect
- Client-facing visibility layer for approved client-safe exam status updates

Zoho / CRM
- Future source of truth for client, account, contact, application, qualifier, and service engagement records

WorkDrive
- Storage layer for exam-related documents, score reports, transcripts, receipts, and board correspondence

Command Center / RoseOS
- Leadership view for high-risk exam blockers, overdue follow-ups, and application dependencies

Audit whether these ecosystem boundaries are clear and whether the app avoids becoming a duplicate source of truth.

## 6. Automation Audit

Identify whether safe automations exist or should exist.

Safe internal automations:

- Flag exam not scheduled after eligibility confirmed
- Flag study readiness not confirmed before scheduled exam
- Remind owner before scheduled exam date
- Create follow-up when result is pending after exam date
- Create transcript task after exam marked passed
- Flag transcript not ordered after pass
- Flag transcript pending too long
- Mark related applications as potentially unblocked after transcript received
- Create client update draft after major milestone
- Add RoseOS/Command Center blocker item for high-risk exam delays
- Alert owner when retake is required

Human review required:

- Sending client/candidate messages
- Publishing ComplianceConnect updates
- Updating official CRM/Zoho records
- Marking application officially unblocked
- Making exam eligibility determinations
- Making legal/compliance conclusions
- Telling client an application will be approved
- Guaranteeing timelines
- Sending agency/board communications

Audit whether the app clearly separates safe internal automation from human-reviewed external actions.

## 9. Risk and Compliance Audit

Check for unsafe or misleading language.

The app must not imply:

- Exams are guaranteed to be passed
- Board approval is guaranteed
- Application approval is guaranteed
- Timelines are guaranteed
- CCA can control agency/board processing
- Legal advice has been given
- Official agency submission happened if it did not
- Client-facing updates were published if only created locally
- CRM/Zoho was updated if it was not

Preferred safe language:

- Draft created
- Human review required
- Ready for review
- Pending agency/board confirmation
- Pending candidate action
- Pending transcript confirmation
- Local preview only
- Sync-ready
- External sending disabled in this build

## Executive Summary
Give a direct summary of whether ExamsManagerOS is ready, partially ready, or not ready.

## Overall Readiness Score
Score from 0 to 100.

Use this scoring:
- 90-100: Ready for internal pilot
- 75-89: Strong but needs cleanup
- 60-74: Useful prototype, missing key workflows
- 40-59: Concept exists but not operationally ready
- 0-39: Mostly incomplete or cosmetic

## What Already Works
List working areas with evidence from the app/code.

## Critical Missing Items
List anything that must be added before internal use.

## Workflow Gaps
Cover exam requirement, eligibility, scheduling, results, transcripts, blocked applications, client updates, and escalations.

## Data Model Gaps
List missing objects, fields, relationships, or persistence issues.

## UI / UX Gaps
List design, navigation, responsiveness, readability, and usability issues.

## Automation Gaps
List safe automations missing and human-review boundaries missing.

## Integration Gaps
Break down by:
- Doc Collection
- ComplianceConnect
- Zoho / CRM
- WorkDrive
- RoseOS / Command Center

## Compliance / Safety Risks
List any language or behavior that creates risk.

## Dead Buttons / Fake Functionality
Identify buttons, links, forms, filters, or screens that appear functional but do not work.

## Recommended Build Fixes
Prioritize into:
1. Must fix now
2. Should fix before pilot
3. Nice to have later

## Suggested Revised Architecture
Give a better structure if needed.

## Suggested Screen List
Give the final recommended screen/sidebar structure.

## Suggested Data Objects
Give the final recommended data model.

## Acceptance Tests
Provide a checklist the app must pass before Rose reviews it.

## Final Recommendation
Give a plain-English recommendation:
- approve for internal testing
- fix before testing
- rebuild workflow
- needs architecture review

Be direct, specific, and do not sugarcoat missing functionality.
