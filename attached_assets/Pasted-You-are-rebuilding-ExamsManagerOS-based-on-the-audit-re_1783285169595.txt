You are rebuilding ExamsManagerOS based on the audit report.

Do not treat the current app as ready. Treat it as a polished frontend shell that needs to become a real internal RoseOS operating app.

The audit found that ExamsManagerOS currently looks good, but does not model the actual exam lifecycle. It is missing eligibility, study/prep, registration, results, transcripts, related applications, tasks, escalations, client-update review, audit events, and persistence. It also has fake/dead buttons and misleading integration labels.

Your job is to upgrade the app into a functional protected-preview operating system.

App name:

ExamsManagerOS

Positioning:

ExamsManagerOS is a RoseOS Ecosystem app for managing exam requirements, exam readiness, exam scheduling, exam results, transcripts, and application blockers for Contractor Compliance Authority.

This app supports licensing and compliance operations where exams and transcripts can block license applications, reciprocity applications, qualifier approvals, and client progress.

The app must make it immediately clear:

- Who needs to take an exam
- What exam is required
- Which board/state/application requires it
- Whether the candidate is eligible
- Whether study/prep is complete
- Whether the exam is scheduled
- Whether the exam was passed or failed
- Whether the official transcript was ordered
- Whether the transcript was received
- Which applications are blocked by the exam/transcript
- Who owns the next action
- What is overdue, stalled, or high risk
